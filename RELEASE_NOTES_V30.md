# BunkWise v2.1.7-26 Release Notes

Welcome to **BunkWise v2.1.7-26** (Build 30)! 📱⚡🚀

This release focuses on streamlining document interaction and removing visual artifacts from the startup sequence.

---

## 🚀 What's New

### 1. Inbuilt Document Viewer Bypassed 📂
* Bypassed the custom in-app viewer completely. Clicking on any document card in Notes or Syllabus now directly calls the system-native Android app chooser, letting you choose your preferred office or PDF reader instantly.

---

### 2. Clean Launch Sequence (No FAB Flash) 🧹
* Removed the redundant floating action button (`+` button) from the DOM. This resolves the split-second floating button flash that appeared on the bottom-right during launch before subject data loaded.

---

## 🛠️ Under-the-Hood Updates

### 1. Version Update 📦
* Updated `versionName` to `"2.1.7-26"`.
* Updated `versionCode` to `30`.
