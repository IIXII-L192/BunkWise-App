## ❓ What is BunkWise?

Managing attendance requirements while balancing coursework, projects, and personal time is a constant challenge for students. **BunkWise** is built to solve this. 

It acts as a personal attendance assistant, helping you keep track of your classes, schedule your week, and calculate exactly where you stand relative to your minimum attendance targets (like the standard **75%** threshold).

---

## 💡 What Does BunkWise Do?

### 1. Launcher-Themed Home View
* **Asymmetric Grid Layout**: A sleek, beautifully stacked 2-column home launcher featuring 3D illustrations.
* **Consolidated Quick Launch**: Tap tiles to open Timetable, Subjects, Simulator, Notes, Uploads, and Syllabus views.
* **Premium Typography**: Rendered in modern Clash Grotesk and Satoshi geometric fonts.

### 2. Smart Attendance Calculations
* **Know When to "Bunk"**: Tells you exactly how many upcoming classes you can afford to miss (*bunk*) while staying safely above your target percentage.
* **Know When to Attend**: If your attendance drops, BunkWise calculates the exact number of consecutive classes you must attend to recover and cross the threshold.
* **Theory vs. Practical Separation**: Tracks *Theory (PP)* and *Practical (PR)* statistics independently, acknowledging that different courses have distinct attendance requirements.

### 3. Sleek Timetable Scheduling
* **Weekly Overview**: Displays your daily classes in a clean visual schedule.
* **Dynamic Time Highlights**: Draws current class indicator highlights in real-time, helping you see at a glance which class is active right now.
* **Free-Slot (Gap) Finder**: Identifies gaps between your classes and suggests breaks (e.g., *"Perfect for a quick break ☕"*, *"Time for lunch 🍔"*).
* **Mark Holidays & Off-days**: Mark days as weekly off-days or trigger a temporary "Holiday Today" state to halt your daily attendance reminders.

### 4. Double-Tap Semesters & Roman Count Picker
* **Roman Numeral Indicators**: The Semester card on the home screen displays the active semester count in Roman numerals (e.g. `V`).
* **Spinner Selector Wheel**: Creating a new semester uses a clean spinner picker from `1` to `16` (rendered as `Semester I` to `Semester XVI`), rather than requiring a text entry.
* **Double-Tap Flow**: Toggle easily between start name selection and archive confirmation.

### 5. Class End Alarms & Notifications
* **Baggage Alerts**: Delivers a notification exactly **10 minutes before** a class is set to end, reminding you: *"Pack yer bags, class ends in 10 minutes!"*.
* **Custom Daily Log Reminders**: Alerters prompt you to update your daily attendance, customizable to trigger at any hour of your choice (e.g., 7:00 PM) using an interactive clock interface.

### 6. Interactive Absence Analytics
* **Skip Distribution Heatmap**: A visual dashboard bar chart displaying absences over the week, so you can easily spot which weekdays you tend to skip classes most frequently.
* **Balance Monitor**: Analyzes your course logs and informs you if your attendance habits are balanced or leaning heavily towards one class format (*Theory Heavy* vs. *Prac Heavy*).

### 7. Instant Offline Schedule Sharing
* **Peer-to-Peer Import/Export**: Export your timetable to an ultra-compressed QR code with a centered logo. A classmate can scan the QR code to instantly copy the schedule to their phone—100% offline, with no account creation or internet required.
* **Import Previews**: Restoring from a backup or importing via QR code shows a clean list of what's inside (subjects count, logs, and timetables) so you can confirm changes before applying them.
